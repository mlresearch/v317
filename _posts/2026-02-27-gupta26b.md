title: 'FedNeuro: Multi-Site fMRI Analysis Using Hypernetwork Personalized and Privacy
  Enhanced Federated Learning'
abstract: 'Multi-site functional MRI (fMRI) studies enable comprehensive understanding
  of brain disorders by integrating data across institutions, yet centralized model
  training remains limited by privacy regulations and domain heterogeneity. We propose
  FedNeuro, a hypernetwork-personalized and privacy-enhanced federated learning framework
  for collaborative fMRI analysis. Unlike conventional federated averaging, FedNeuro
  employs a global hypernetwork that generates site-specific model parameters from
  private client embeddings, allowing each site to learn personalized representations
  while maintaining global consistency. This bi-level meta-optimization decouples
  data-dependent gradients from shared parameters, providing structural privacy protection
  and improving cross-site generalization. Evaluated on the multi-site ABIDE dataset,
  FedNeuro achieves robust gains over federated baselines, marking a step toward scalable,
  privacy-preserving, and domain-fair neuroimaging for precision neuroscience. Code:
  https://github.com/sunnyinAI/FedNeuro'
